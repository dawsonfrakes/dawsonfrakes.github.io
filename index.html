Oddly applicable life lessons learned from Rocket League

  • Being Top 0.1% at something and being #1 is the same difference as Top 0.1% and Top 50%.
  • 10000 hours isn't enough to master something.
  • Consistent repetition and focused improvements are how to master something.
  • Time off for your brain to process is just as important to learning a skill as intense practice.
  • Sometimes taking it back is the best way to progress.
  • Never give up until it's over.
  • If you know your opponent, it's often better to play them than the game.
  • Faking something can result in a better outcome than doing something.
